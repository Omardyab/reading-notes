## ES6 Syntax and Feature Overview


* ECMAScript 2015, also known as ES6, introduced many changes to JavaScript.    
* Here is a key of most identifier names used throughout this reference.

    - Variable: **x**
    - Object: **obj**
    - Array: **arr**
    - Function: **func**
    - Parameter, method: **a**, **b**, **c**
    - String: **str**
